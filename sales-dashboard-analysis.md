## Problem
Sales leadership lacked visibility into regional performance and product trends. Reports were manual and delayed.

## Objective
Create a structured dashboard to track KPIs and identify underperforming regions.

## Data Used
- Monthly sales dataset (50,000 rows)
- Region, product, revenue, customer segment

## Approach
- Cleaned data in Excel
- Removed duplicates and missing values
- Created pivot tables for KPI tracking
- Built dashboard with charts and slicers
- Defined KPI formulas

## KPIs Designed
- Revenue by Region
- Product Category Performance
- Monthly Growth %
- Top Customers

## Insights
- Region West underperformed by 18%
- 2 products generated 60% revenue
- Seasonal spikes in Q3

## Business Impact
- Enabled faster decision making
- Reduced manual reporting time by 40%
- Helped target low-performing regions

## Tools Used
Excel, Pivot Tables, Charts
